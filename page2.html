Key Contacts

In order to ensure your event runs smoothly, we need a key point of contact if any changes to your event itinerary are required on the day due to delays or any unforeseen happenings. We advise that your key contact is not a main person on the event day, such as the bride or groom for wedding events.

The main point of contact will need to be completely familiar with the details in this form and are to have ideally visited the venue before. It is advised for them to be present at the meeting.

Primary Point of Contact

In the case of a joint day, we require contact details of a main point of contact from both the bride's side & groom's side. If this applies to your event, please provide details for another main point of contact from the other side.

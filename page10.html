Catering

If your chosen caterer has not worked at Amington Hall before they will need to organise a site visit with the Venue. It is vital that the caterer inspects the kitchen facilities on site and understands how our kitchen equipment is operated well before your event.

Please note: the Venue does NOT provide any serving dishes. These will need to be provided by your chosen caterer and be white in colour. Please see our Party Catering leaflet for more information.

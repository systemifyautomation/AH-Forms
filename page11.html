Car Parking

There are 3 key areas of parking for our Venue and designated as:

  • Section 1: Venue Level - 6 VIP + 14 Priority
  • Section 2: Top Level – 16 Priority
  • Section 3: Bottom Level Main Car parking area for overflow guests. NO PASSES REQUIRED

VIP Parking – directly in front of the main entrance to the Venue with 6 parking spaces

Priority Parking - close to the entrance of the Venue with 30 parking spaces

Main Parking – car parking spaces adjacent to the Venue

We recommend that you reserve VIP/Venue level for the groom side, and the Top level for Bride side if you are planning to have a baraat/grooms side arrival separately.

We recommend that you reserve Priority Parking spaces close family, special guests or elderly guests.

For all reserved parking we will provide you with a set number of passes that you will need to hand-out prior to your event day. Guests with no reserved parking passes will be asked to park in the Main Parking Spaces.
